Hassle-Free Neurology Treatment Plan

At Artemis Hospitals, we're dedicated to giving our patients a smooth and easy experience. Our all-inclusive care plans are made to guarantee open communication, accessibility, and individualized care that takes into account all facets of your neurological health. From diagnosis to recovery, we help you navigate each step of your care, ensuring a seamless and effective experience. Our team of experts performs a comprehensive assessment of your ailment at the initial consultation and diagnosis, marking the start of the journey. We then develop a personalized treatment plan based on the results, taking into account your unique requirements. Advanced imaging and testing are then performed to obtain more information and guarantee the most precise diagnosis. We proceed with continuing monitoring and rehabilitation to assist your healing and well-being after the required data has been obtained. With many follow-up appointments, we guarantee ongoing care throughout your course of treatment, ensuring that your health is regularly assessed and that any necessary adjustments are made.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of neurological conditions does Artemis treat?

+

Brain strokes, brain tumors, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, Alzheimer's, multiple sclerosis, headaches and migraines, neurogenetic disorders, and neuromuscular diseases are just a few of the neurological problems for which Artemis Hospitals provides specialist care. Both common and complicated disorders affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system are treated individually by our skilled neurologists.

Does Artemis require a reference before I can see a neurologist?

+

No, in order to make an appointment with a neurologist at Artemis Hospitals, a recommendation is not necessary. By phoning our helpline or scheduling an appointment online via our website, you can speak with one of our specialists immediately. For our patients, we make the procedure as simple as easy as we can.

How do I get an appointment with a neurologist?

+

By phoning our helpline or using our website's handy online booking system, you can quickly schedule an appointment with one of our knowledgeable neurologists. Our staff will confirm your appointment after you choose a time and date that works for you. We want every patient to have a hassle-free experience.

How much does Artemis neurological treatment cost?

+

Depending on the precise ailment, diagnosis, and treatment strategy needed, Artemis' neurological therapy costs vary. The cost is affected by variables such the type of therapy, length of hospital stay, and diagnostic testing. We invite you to visit our website or get in touch with our billing department for comprehensive pricing details.

Can international patients avail treatment at Artemis ?

+

Indeed, Artemis Hospitals offers a smooth experience from consultation to recuperation and welcomes patients from across the world. Our committed staff of foreign patient services can help with lodging, travel planning, and visa requirements. For patients receiving treatment abroad, we make sure that all required assistance is given to make the process as easy and comfortable as possible.

How long will it take me to receive neurological treatment at the hospital?

+

The severity of your ailment, the complexity of the necessary treatment, and your rate of recovery will all affect how long you stay at Artemis Hospitals for neurological care. Our medical staff will provide an anticipated duration of stay based on your individual treatment plan following an initial consultation and diagnostic evaluation.

Is there a network of support for care after treatment?

+

Yes, in order to assist patients in their recuperation, Artemis Hospitals provides a thorough post-treatment rehabilitation program. To guarantee a complete recovery, this also includes counseling, occupational therapy, and physical therapy. In order to track progress and modify the treatment plan as needed, we also offer follow-up care and routine evaluations.

How can I be certain of the doctors' level of expertise?

+

Our neurologists and neurosurgeons at Artemis Hospitals are highly skilled professionals with a track record of successful treatments and significant international training. Numerous of our experts have held positions at esteemed international organizations and remain current with the most recent findings and developments in neurological care. All patients receive excellent care thanks to the experience of our staff.

Which imaging services are offered for the diagnosis of neurological conditions?

+

For neurological diagnostics, Artemis Hospitals provides a variety of sophisticated imaging services, such as PET-CT (Positron Emission Tomography), CT scans, MRIs (Magnetic Resonance Imaging), and neuro-physiological testing like NCV, EEG, and EMG. Our professionals can more properly detect neurological problems and determine the best course of treatment with the use of these diagnostic instruments.

Do you provide services in pediatric neurology?

+

Indeed, the pediatric neurology department at Artemis Hospitals is specialized in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders in children. With a caring approach catered to the special requirements of young patients, our pediatric neurologists offer skilled treatment for a variety of illnesses, such as epilepsy, cerebral palsy, neurogenetic disorders, and more.
